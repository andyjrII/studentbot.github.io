About the Project

The project was created to allow users make their enquiries pertaining to the department of Computer Science, DS Adegbenro ICT Polytechnic, just by interacting with a chat bot through chat.

Users can click on the icon on the home page, which opens up the chat frame. This is a bot & not a human, so you have to be as precise as possible with your enquiries. Users can check their results (only registered students of the department can do this); check details, outline & lecturer in charge of each courses; check details of lecturers in the department; ask questions pertaining to some common computer science terms etc...

The project is designed for the Department of Computer Science, DS Adegbenro ICT Polytechnic, Itori-Ewekoro, Ogun state in partial fulfillment for the requirement of Higher National Diploma in Computer Science.
